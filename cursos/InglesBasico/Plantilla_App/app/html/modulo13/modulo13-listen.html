Listen and Repeat

Introducción

En esta sección escucharás conversaciones de diferentes personas y situaciones, para que te familiarices con el idioma. No te preocupes si no entiendes cada una de las palabras, ¡trata de comprender qué es lo que quieren decir!
Si lo deseas, puedes apoyarte con un diccionario para buscar las palabras clave en cada conversación. ¡Adelante, hazlo, tú puedes! Es uno de los ejercicios más recomendados para aprender a expresarte oralmente en inglés.

Listening

Diálogo 1

Going to a Restaurant / Yendo a un restaurante

Going to a Restaurant / Yendo a un restaurante

Laura: Mom, I’m hungry. Let’s go out and eat.
Dad: I’m hungry, too.
Mom: Great idea! What would you like to eat?
Laura: I would like to eat spaghetti and meatballs.
Dad: I would like to eat a cheeseburger and fries.
Mom: I would like to eat soup and salad.
Dad: Everyone wants to eat something different.
Laura: I know what we can do. Let’s go to the “Pig-Pen” Restaurant. They have everything we like.
Mom: That’s right. They have spaghetti and meatballs for Laura, cheeseburgers for Dad, and soup and salad for me. That’s a great choice. Let’s go!

Diálogo 2

Supermarket / Supermercado

Supermarket / Supermercado

Mari: I need to go the supermarket. Do you want to come with me?
Beatriz: What do you need to buy?
Mari: A little bit of everything! We need milk and eggs. We need butter. We need bread.
Beatriz: We have run out of cereal, too.
Mari: Okay. We also need meat, fruit and many vegetables.
Beatriz: Wow. That’s a lot of food! Maybe we should ask Dad to come with us so he can help carry the bags!
Mari: Good idea!

Diálogo 3

Ordering food in a Restaurant / Ordenando comida en un restaurante

Ordering food in a Restaurant / Ordenando comida en un restaurante

Bill: Yes, I would like a glass of milk.
Server: Would you like some dessert?
Angie: I would like the brownie and ice cream.
Bill: I want some apple pie.
Server: Okay. I’ll be back with your beverages.
Bill: Excuse me, could you bring me a cup of coffee with my pie?
Server: Yes, of course.
